Toast

This is a time-honored family recipe

Quick and very simple way to curb your late night hunger. One of the most versatile meals you can make

Ingredients

  • 30 grams of sliced bread(10cm x 11cm x 1cm)
  • 5 Tablespoons butter

  1. If you have a top loading toaster

    1. Take one piece of toast and gently place into top loading toaster
    2. Take a second piece of toast and gently place into top loading toaster

  2. If you have a toaster oven

    1. Take piece of bread in right or left hand.
    2. With alternate hand, open toaster- NOTE: This step may vary depending on your toaster oven.
    3. Place piece of bread in toaster oven.

  3. For both appliances

    1. Turn dial/knob determine the level of darkness to your desire
    2. [Assuming your toaster oven is plugged into a working electric socket] Turn on toaster
    3. Wait untill appliance completes operation
    4. remove toast(No longer bread) when the toasting of said toast is no longer toasting.
    5. Place toast on surface of object of your choice.
    6. Take nife in hand.
    7. Using nife, cut 4-7 tablespoons of butter.
    8. Spread said butter on toast.
    9. Eat.
