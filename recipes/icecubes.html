How to make Ice Cubes when the ice tray is empty

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups water from faucet
  • 2 tablespoons water from faucet

Directions

  1. Take empty ice tray and place flat on counter
  2. Gently pour over glasses of water over ice try, ensuring that each block is fill
  3. Use spoons of water to top up any ice block
  4. Place the water filled ice tray back into the freezer
  5. Shut the freezer door
  6. Be sure to leave for around 4-6 hours at least to ensure it is sufficiently frozen
